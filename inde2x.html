Free & open to all

German Book Club 📚

A small, friendly group meeting once a week to read German together — no pressure, no teacher, just people helping each other learn. We read simplified A2/B1 books out loud, do the exercises together, and chat about what we've read. All levels welcome as long as you're curious! 🙂

How it works

📖
We read together We read the book out loud as a group — no need to prepare anything in advance.
✏️
We do the exercises Each book comes with comprehension and grammar exercises — we work through them together, helping each other out.
💬
We discuss We talk about the story, the vocabulary, anything that comes up — mix of German and English, whatever helps. Completely community-driven, no instructor.
